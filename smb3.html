HISTORY

“Super Mario Bros. 3 (often known as Mario 3, SMB3, or Super Mario 3) is the third game in the Super Mario series. The game was released in 1988, and was developed and published by Nintendo. It was released in Japan on October 23, 1988; in North America on February 9, 1990; and in Europe and Australia on August 29, 1991. Super Mario Bros. 3 has been considered as one of the greatest games of all time. Its complexity and challenging levels made it a huge success.”
